Skillet Lasagna

Ingredient Name Quantity
Olive Oil 2 Tbs.
Onion 1 medium
Garlic (minced) 2 cloves
Ground Beef 1 lb
Marinara Sauce 24 oz.
Italian Seasoning 1 Tbs.
Cottage Cheese 1 cup
Fresh Flat-Leaf Parsley 3/4 cup
Salt and Pepper Personal preference
Mozzarella (grated, divided) 2 cups
Lasagna Noodles 8 pcs.

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Heat the oil in a cast-iron skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and saute 5-6 mins til onion is soft, add the garlic and cook a further minute.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook 4-5 mins until no longer pink.
  4. Add the pasta sauce and Italian Seasoning and simmer, stirring occasionally around 5-6 mins.
  5. Transfer 2/3 of the meat sauce to a bowl, leaving 1/3 the sauce in the skillet
  6. Lay half the noodles over the sauce in the skillet, spoon half the cottage cheese mixture over the noodles, sprinkle 1/2 cup of mozzarella, then repeat with sauce, noodles, cottage cheese,1/2 cup mozzarella and last 1/3 of the sauce.
  7. Cover the pan tightly with foil and bake until the noodles are tender, 25 to 30 mins.
  8. Remove from the oven, and let it rest a few minutes â€“ broiling the top if desired.
  9. Garnish with chopped parsley.
  10. Devour!

A delicious lasagna that is made in a cast-iron skillet. This comfort food classic is perfect for weeknights and pleases the whole family!
